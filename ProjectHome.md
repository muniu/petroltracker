A web and SMS-based system for receiving, sending and tracking petrol/gas prices within Nairobi. You can then schedule to fill-up at specific stations, knowing they are the cheapest within a particular range.

In addition to the Twitter and Blog links on the right sidebar, you can also join us on any or all of these project mailing-lists:

Discussions: http://groups.google.com/group/petroltracker-discuss

Developers: http://groups.google.com/group/petroltracker-devel
